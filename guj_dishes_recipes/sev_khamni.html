Sev khamni Recipe

Watch video

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves
  • salt as required
  • 2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • refined oil as required
  • 500 gm chana dal
  • 4 tablespoon pomegranate seeds
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 2 teaspoon crushed to paste green chilli
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 500 gm sev
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
+

How to make Sev khamni

Step 1

To prepare this Gujarati specialty, first take a large sized bowl and pour good amount of water in it. Then, transfer the chana dal in it. Cover with a lid and keep aside for at least 5-6 hours.

Step 2

Next, take a large sized pan and place it on medium-low flame. Then, pour and heat little amount of oil in it. Now, add mustard seeds and stir them for 5-10 seconds. Next, add ginger paste along with green chilli paste, sugar, lemon juice and mix well. Also, add salt as required. Stir well for about 10-12 minutes and then keep aside.

Step 3

After the dal is soaked in water for at least 5-6 hours, drain the water from its bowl. Now, transfer the soaked dal in a mixer-grinder jar. Grind it at maximum speed until it becomes partially smooth in texture.

Step 4

Next, take a separate large sized pan and place it on medium flame. Now, transfer the blended dal in the pan and stir well until it reaches the boiling temperature. Make sure that the dal is cooked properly and is tender in texture. Also, add salt as required and mix well. Once done, turn the flame off and keep aside.

Step 5

Now, take a serving dish and transfer the prepared dal(step 4) in it. Then, transfer the prepared spice mixture(step 2) over it. Garnish with pomegranate, sev, coriander leaves and serve immediately.
